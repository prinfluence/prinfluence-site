Prin.

Founder, Prinfluence — AI marketing for creators and brands.

I build AI-powered marketing systems for brands — and I teach creators how to use the same tools to grow.

Prinfluence started with a simple frustration: marketing was still being done by hand, slowly, while AI was quietly changing what a small team could actually produce. I wanted to close that gap — for the brands I work with and for the creators figuring it out on their own.

So I built both sides. An agency that designs AI content systems for travel, lifestyle and tech brands. And a set of digital products that give creators the same edge — better pitches, smarter outreach, fairer rates.

These days I split my time between client work, building tools, and a YouTube channel where I show both how to actually use AI — not just talk about it. Same playbook, two audiences.

When I'm not in a doc or on camera, I'm probably outside somewhere in Maryland, planning the next thing.
